MUCKY DRIVER

About Mucky Driver

Mucky Driver is a game where you upgrade through various cars and vehicles by collecting Points, Gems and CustomPoints all over the map. Furthermore, you can complete in-game challenges which give you something else to do while playing the game which will reward you for completing it. Missions can be completed by going through various different stages where you have to find the fastest and the best possible way past the obstacles to finish the mission succesfully. Learn More

The Story of Raven and Muck County

You have been invited to the Mucky Driver Group where they tell you the story about how Raven and Muck County was created! Everyone who had lived before had to experience the most devastating natural disaster ever recorded in that area. The Tsunami took out most of the building's structure and damaged the shore near the town. After the disaster the government which was in charge at the time (Raven County Government) had to stop maintaining half of their land where it became abandoned for many years after the disaster. Mucky Driver Group was founded by a person who believed that the land left over should be re-claimed however for a different use. Full Story
