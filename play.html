Mucky Driver Game

Mucky Driver Remastered 2021

DHCGamer1055 (aka Claasgreeneye), Linksh

Mucky Driver is a offroad styled game where you play with numerous amount of different varieties of vehicles. Explore the large vast world which is now in the ruins from the past natural disaster which was a tsunami! It is your job to explore the land, and take ownership of all the vehicles. However, don't forget about the obsticles around the map, there can be sticky mud puddles and deadly waters which can drown your vehicles. The choice is yours how you play the game: There are multiple missions to be completed, to then be awarded if successfully completing one of them.
