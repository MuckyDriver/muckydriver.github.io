Cars

There are currently 16 cars in the game and 15 which can be unlocked! The other 1 is part of VIP which is the Monster Truck. The monster truck is capable of knocking down trees in its path when it is at a fast speed. Also, there is 1 car pack which is the Pickup Car pack which inlcudes 3 different colour designs and max speeds.

How to report a bug?

To report a bug you must be joined to our discord server!
Here are the steps on how to make a report on a bug for the game or the website:

1. Firstly go to the channel named #bug-reporting in category Other

2. Inside that channel do a command !bug-report {game or website} {lighting, graphics, ui, controls, sound etc...} {detailed info of the bug you're experiencing}
3. In a couple of hours to days your bug report may be responded to by our staff or we will approve it!
